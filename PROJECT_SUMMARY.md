# Global Empowerment Platform (GEP) - Project Summary

## üéØ What We're Building

**Global Empowerment Platform (GEP)** is a social growth engine that transforms 8,000+ members into funded entrepreneurs through:

### Two Primary Objectives:

1. **Grow Every Member's Digital Influence**
   - Raise follower counts
   - Strengthen brand identity
   - Increase content output
   - Become visible on FB, IG, TikTok, YouTube
   - Build real communities around their idea/mission
   - Develop a digital presence that VC firms validate

2. **Prepare Members for Capital Investment**
   - Train members on business formation
   - Help them build traction
   - Score them using a Funding Readiness Score (0-100)
   - Match them with VCs, lenders, angel investors
   - Highlight top-performing members
   - Showcase real progress using AI analytics

## üìä Funding Readiness Score Breakdown

The score (0-100) is calculated from:
- **Posting Frequency** (15 points) - How often they post
- **Brand Clarity** (10 points) - Business name, bio completeness
- **Business Model** (15 points) - Products, services defined
- **Community Engagement** (20 points) - Likes, comments, shares received
- **Follower Growth** (15 points) - Follower count and growth
- **Revenue Signals** (10 points) - Products with pricing
- **Product Catalog** (10 points) - Number of products
- **Pitch Deck** (5 points) - Pitch deck completion

**Status Levels:**
- **0-49:** Building
- **50-79:** Emerging
- **80-100:** VC-Ready
